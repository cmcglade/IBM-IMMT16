About Me

David Steele is a multimedia producer based in Aurora, Canada. An alumnus of McMaster University's Multimedia and History programs, he is currently attending Centennial College, focusing on Interactive Media Managment. The creator of the FIRST "Nisekoi Abridged" series on YouTube, as well as a co-founder of the OTALKU Podcast; David is capable of producing, editing and consulting on Multimedia related projects.


Additional work

In addition to entertainment related multimedia, David has produced content relating to legally supporting content creators called "Supporting the Official Release" (#STOR), as well as a conceptual advertising program titled the "Informed Voter Campaign".


Recent activities

David has been active in media production since September 2009. As a student, he was the primary videographer for Villanova College from February 2010 to June 2011. He returned to Villanova College as a volunteer Marketing assistant and Media Consolidation specialist in September 2015.
He co-ordinated the recording of the school's annual arts event "Arts Knight", and edited over 200 GB of footage together to create a proper representation of the performances students put on. Utilizing footage from three different cameras presented a unique challenge that the school had never attemtped to work with.
