a bit about me

creative . clear . concise



I am a girl in a studio creating things. Illustrating, painting, drawing, designing.

I approach design with the eye of an artist. After studying fine art and working as a painter and artist for more than a decade I was drawn to the digital landscape by the potential for beautiful, clean design and the opportunity to create meaningful, simplistic and intuitive user experiences. I believe an artist is an artist whatever their chosen medium.

I work from my home studio in Toronto's west end and blog my musings whenever the mood hits.
